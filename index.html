Welcome to Zoom Media Tucson.

Zoom Media Connect matches local businesses with an upscale and active audience in health clubs, fitness centers and recreational venues across the country. How do we do it? First, we start with a network of the best known and most respected venues in the country. Gold’s Gym, LA Fitness, Planet Fitness, Bally Total Fitness, JCCs/YMCAs and numerous regional and local leaders all broadcast our network.

Grab Attention

We hold the attention of the members at the club by broadcasting a combination of entertainment content, relevant and timely fitness programming, advertisements and “news members can use” to get the most out of their membership.

Why Health Clubs?

Health club members are a desirable audience for local businesses since they outspend other groups of consumers in categories like restaurants, home improvement, retail, medical, insurance, automotive, financial services and many more.

The results is a unique, cost efficient and powerful communications medium that connects local businesses to customers and prospective customers who live and work in the community.
